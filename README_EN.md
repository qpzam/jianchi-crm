## Product Positioning

JianChi CRM Pro is an intelligent lead generation system designed for block trade institutions and restricted share buyers. It automatically monitors A-share shareholder reduction announcements via AI, extracts key information, matches contact details, and helps sales teams reach potential clients faster than competitors.
